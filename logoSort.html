Inspired by the Journey Challenge in Survivor Season 46 Episode 5

How to Play:

  • Arrange the Survivor season logos in the order of their original airdate from left to right, top to bottom to win
  • Press Play Shared Game to arrange the same set of seasons shared
  • Press New Easy Game to arrange 9 random seasons
  • Press New Standard Game to arrange 20 random seasons
  • Press Journey Challenge to arrange the same set of seasons from the episode

How to Move Tiles:

  • Click (or tap) on the logo tile you wish to move
  • Hold and drag the tile to its correct position on the grid
  • Release the tile to lock it into place

Good luck!
