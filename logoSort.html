Survivor Logo Sorting Challenge

Inspired by the Journey Challenge in Survivor Season 46 Episode 5

How to Play

  • Arrange the Survivor season logos in the order of their original airdate to win
  • The game board is arranged with the first season at the top-left and the last season at the bottom-right
  • To move a tile click (or tap), hold and drag
  • Press 'Play' to arrange 20 random seasons
  • Press 'Journey Challenge' to arrange the same set of seasons from the episode

Thank you for playing!
